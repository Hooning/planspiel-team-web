Our Values

Culture of our team.

Improvement

Whenever we face the problem, always discuss and find a better way to solve it. If we are reluctant to try new things, we will never able to improve ourselves and our product will not full fill the customer's needs enough.

Collaborative

Team power comes from effective communication and collaborative attitude.

    Self-organized attitude - Do not wait until the other team members ask you to do.

    Share issues quickly - Do not hesitate to ask something which will affect other problems in the later stage.

    Retrospect - Let's review what we have done wrong, mistakes can be improved.
